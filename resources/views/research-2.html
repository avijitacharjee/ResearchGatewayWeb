For people in social sciences and other fields, the most commonly used type of research is often qualitative research. Qualitative Research is a vital part of any social science research paper. It implies formulating a hypothesis on the basis of study of the sample group. Research Gateway has an experienced team for conducting qualitative research. Key features of qualitative research:

Diversity and perspectives of the participants.

Variety of methods and approaches.
